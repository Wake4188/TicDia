TicDia

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026  ·  Effective date: February 20, 2026

TL;DR: We collect only what we need to run TicDia. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

1. Who We Are

TicDia is an independent knowledge application that presents Wikipedia content in an engaging, short-form format. For privacy-related inquiries, please use our in-app contact form (found in the footer or profile settings).

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (to identify your account and send security notifications)
  • Hashed password (via Supabase Auth — we never see your plaintext password)
  • Birth year (optional — used only to gate age-restricted social features)

Preferences & Personalization

  • Reading preferences: font, highlight color, text size, background opacity, language
  • Feature toggles: smoke effect, text animation, liquid glass mode, adult content filter
  • TTS speed and feed preference (random / curated / mixed)

Reading Activity

  • Articles you save (title, URL, timestamp)
  • Reading analytics: articles viewed, scroll distance, streaks, audio listening time
  • Votes you cast on articles
  • Daily challenge progress and earned achievements

Technical Data

  • Browser type and device category (via Vercel Analytics — anonymized)
  • General geographic region (country-level, not precise location)
  • Page visit timestamps and session data

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance: Processing required to provide the service (account management, preferences, saved articles)
  • Legitimate interests: Service improvement, security monitoring, and abuse prevention
  • Consent: Cookie preferences and optional analytics tracking

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver and personalize the TicDia experience
  • Maintain reading streaks, challenges, and achievement badges
  • Send essential account notifications (email verification, password reset)
  • Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access
  • Improve the platform based on aggregate (not individual) usage patterns

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We use the following sub-processors:

  • Supabase (EU-hosted option available) — Database, authentication, and file storage
  • Vercel — Web hosting and privacy-first analytics (no fingerprinting, GDPR-compliant)
  • ElevenLabs — Text-to-speech audio generation (article text sent to their API, no personal data)
  • Wikipedia / Wikimedia Foundation — Article content (governed by their own privacy policy)

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Database access is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) — your data is only accessible to you
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth
  • API keys are stored as encrypted server-side secrets, never in client code
  • Regular security reviews are conducted

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Analytics data older than 1 year and vote data older than 2 years are automatically purged. Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

AccessRequest a copy of your personal data
RectificationCorrect inaccurate data
ErasureRequest deletion of your account and data
RestrictionLimit how we process your data
PortabilityReceive your data in a structured format
ObjectObject to data processing

To exercise any of these rights, use the Contact form in the app or at the footer of this page.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

We use essential cookies for authentication sessions and local storage to persist your preferences between visits. We use privacy-first analytics (Vercel Analytics) that do not track individuals or use fingerprinting. You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner shown on first visit.

10. Children's Privacy

TicDia is not directed at users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will notify registered users of material changes to this policy via email with at least 14 days' notice before changes take effect.

13. Supervisory Authority

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., CNIL in France, ICO in the UK).

14. Contact

For all privacy inquiries, GDPR requests, or data deletion requests, please use our in-app contact form — no email address required.
