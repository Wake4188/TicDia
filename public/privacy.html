Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TicDia. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Data Controller

TicDia is the data controller responsible for your personal data. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: noa.wilhide@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

  • Email address (when you create an account)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Profile preferences (font, colors, reading settings)

3.2 Usage Data

  • Articles you read and save
  • Reading time and scroll distance
  • Topics you explore
  • Audio articles you listen to
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location data

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You provide consent when creating an account and accepting cookies
  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our services
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our service and user experience

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Personalize your reading experience
  • Track reading analytics and achievements
  • Send important service notifications
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with the following measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal obligations.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at jessica.wilhide@gmail.com

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner.

10. Third-Party Services

These services have their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions.

12. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.
